Here are the **most common and important features** found in modern **Hospital/Hospital Management Information Systems (HMIS / HIS)** in 2025:

### Core / Must-Have Modules
| #  | Module                          | Main Features / Key Functions                                                                 |
|----|---------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | Patient Registration & OPD      | New/old patient registration, Aadhaar/EMR lookup, UHID/MR number generation, token/slot booking, quick registration, demographic & insurance data capture |
| 2  | Doctor Consultation (OPD/IPD)   | EMR / progress notes, vital signs, complaints, diagnosis (ICD-10), clinical templates, past visit history, allergy & chronic illness flags |
| 3  | Appointment & Queue Management  | Online/offline booking, doctor schedule, slot management, walk-in handling, SMS/WhatsApp reminders, queue display, token calling |
| 4  | Inpatient (IPD) Management      | Bed/ward occupancy, IPD registration, transfer/discharge, bed blocking, daily treatment chart, doctor round notes, diet planning |
| 5  | Nursing & TPR Chart             | Electronic Medication Administration Record (e-MAR), vital charting, intake-output, nursing notes, care plan, handover notes |
| 6  | Laboratory Information System (LIS) | Test order, barcode sample collection, result entry (machine interface + manual), critical value alert, report dispatch (PDF/WhatsApp) |
| 7  | Radiology / PACS Integration    | X-ray, CT, MRI, USG order, DICOM image upload/view, radiologist reporting, report approval workflow, CD burning |
| 8  | Pharmacy (IPD + OPD)            | Drug order from doctor screen, stock deduction, dispensing, return to store, purchase order generation, expiry alert |
| 9  | Billing & Cash Counter          | OPD/IPD billing, advance collection, package / TPA billing, discount management, receipt printing, refund, online payment gateway |
| 10 | Insurance / TPA / Cashless      | Insurance card scanning, eligibility check, pre-auth generation & approval, claim form generation, deduction management, discharge summary for claim |

### Important Supporting / Value-adding Modules
| #  | Module                          | Key Features                                                                                  |
|----|---------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 11 | Operation Theatre (OT) Management | OT scheduling, surgery booking, anaesthesia chart, implant tracking, surgeon & team allocation, OT consumable billing |
| 12 | ICU / Critical Care             | Ventilator & monitor integration, sedation score, Glasgow scale, CRRT/dialysis chart, ABG entry |
| 13 | Blood Bank                      | Donor registration, blood request, cross-match, issue, stock management, transfusion reaction recording |
| 14 | Inventory & Store               | Multi-store management, indent, purchase order, GRN, issue to department, stock expiry alert, ABC/VED analysis |
| 15 | CSSD (Sterile Supply)           | Instrument set tracking, sterilization cycle record, issue/return to OT/wards |
| 16 | Dietary / Food Services         | Diet order from doctor/nurse, diet sheet preparation, food tray tracking, calorie calculation |
| 17 | Discharge Summary & Medico-legal| Auto-fill from EMR, final diagnosis, treatment given, advice on discharge, death summary, MLC register |
| 18 | Electronic Medical Record (EMR) | Longitudinal patient record, document scanning (previous reports), clinical decision support, drug-drug interaction alert |
| 19 | Reports & Analytics             | Doctor performance, department revenue, bed occupancy %, average length of stay, lab turn-around-time, disease pattern, financial MIS |
| 20 | Mobile App / Patient Portal     | Appointment booking, lab/radiology report view, payment, tele-consultation, feedback, medicine reminder |
